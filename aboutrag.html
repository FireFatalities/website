About the Rag

The Fire Cloth




3-D Model




The fire cloth is a multipurpose rag with an outer layer of regular cloth standard to any washrag with an inside made of a fire retardant material. In the rare event of a fire, the rag is single-use. However, the loss is worth it since it saved your valuables, property, and/or your life or wellbeing! The ability to serve as a washrag means that you can use it normally any time, meaning you won't forget that this rag exists any time soon!
The fire cloth is our only product expected to come out soon with hopes to expand our line in the future. The rag costs the cheap* price of $12.49.

*Cheap is relative.
